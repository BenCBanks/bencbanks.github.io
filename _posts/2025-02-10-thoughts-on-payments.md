The payments industry moves fast — and in a lot of directions at once. Between real-time rails, embedded finance, and the ongoing evolution of card networks, there's no shortage of things to keep an eye on.

A few things I'm watching closely:

**Real-time payments** — RTP and FedNow are live, but adoption has been uneven. The interesting question isn't whether real-time payments will win, it's how long it takes for pull payments to catch up with push.

**Embedded finance** — The line between software companies and financial services companies keeps blurring. The platforms that get this right will have a significant advantage.

**Stablecoins** — Still more interesting in theory than in practice for most use cases, but the infrastructure is maturing.

More detailed thoughts on each of these in future posts.
